Who Will Use This Project

The digital portfolio is primarily designed for *students, teachers, and professionals* who want to present projects clearly and interactively.

- *Students* can use this portfolio to showcase their academic or personal projects in a structured manner, making it easier for teachers and peers to review their work.
- *Teachers and educators* can view multiple projects in a standard format, evaluate work efficiently, and provide constructive feedback.
- *Employers or recruiters* can explore the portfolio to see the skills, creativity, and technical knowledge of the person presenting the project.

The portfolio is designed to be *easy to navigate, visually appealing, and interactive*, allowing end users to access all sections – including problem statement, design, features, results, and GitHub link – with minimal effort. This ensures that the audience not only understands the project but also enjoys the experience of exploring it.
